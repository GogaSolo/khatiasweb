Dakishvili family wine
&
chocolate boutique

Dakishvili wine boutique and tasting hall aren’t like any other wine-offering
places you’ve experienced in Telavi.Just Founded in 2023 springtime by the
Dakishvili family, this warm and welcoming wine boutique offers a unique
wine-living atmosphere. In addition, to enjoy time here, be for a time a wine
expert and learn essential details of several styles (antique, classical, dessert,
and sparkling) of wine making and tasting.
Family’s eco-sustainable wines serve up pours at this quaint, hole-in-the-wall
boutique and at a good price, too. Food offerings are limited to snacks, meat,
cheese, Vegan-friendly local sweets, plates of freshly baked bread, and the
best choice of Dakishvili handmade chocolate. Those headings to Dakishvili
wine boutique are there for the hospitality and ambiance, with the possibility
to enjoy limited bottlings of family reserve wines, available only here.

BOUTIQUE ATMOSPHERE

Here is a quiet atmosphere where old wine barrels from the Dakishvili cellar have found a new life, repurposed as tables and armchairs for local wine lovers.
