Dakishvili Wine Cellar, founded in 2002 by Gogi Dakishvili, specializes in producing high-end wines in limited quantities. The Family wine cellars are located in the villages of Shalauri and Kisikhevi in the eastern part of Georgia, in the country's most famous wine region, Kakheti. The region of Kakheti has a deep winemaking tradition and is recognized as the birthplace of Amber skin contact wine technology. Mountains and forests surround the villages of Shalauri and Kisikhevi is one of the Eco cleanest areas in the region. Making wine is a sense of life for the Dakishvili family, which has been making wine for over a century. Each wine series comprises 500 to 5,000 bottles released under the Dakishvili Family Selection brand.
